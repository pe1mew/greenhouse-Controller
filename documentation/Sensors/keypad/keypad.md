Connection to 4x4 keypad is quite simple. 
Connect the 4 columns of the keypad to digital pins 4, 5, 6, and 7 on the Arduino, and the 4 rows to digital pins 8, 9, 10, and 11. This can be done by soldering wires to the keypad or by using connectors or headers to make the connections.
